WHO WE ARE


We are social network for IT. We are young progressive company which create new type of comunication with developer. Our website its non-linear service, which consist of many parts. On this page we want to take the best introduction to our Companies and Jobs board part and how we can create good partnership on background of this two services.

One of the most interesting life moments of our social network its when IT company create its profile and public new jobs. Some companies do this for free promotion, ohers to find good developers to their team. And all of them use our site as comunication platform betweeen company and employer.

Our mission - create website for developers where they can grow, learn and find job, change theirs life. To do this each day we work on creating more productive comunication between our users (companies, recruiters and developers).

Here, at this moment we are looking for partnership with companies, which wants to find new talent persons in the World of developing. Below you can reed our Partnership Map where we display all moments of our mutual benefits.
